About

To create a program that assists in monitoring carbon dioxide emissions and instructs users on how to reduce their consumption. In order to address the issue of emission sustainability, an app with carbon emissions estimates and advice on lowering your carbon footprint might be a useful tool. One of the main ways that the app may help is by increasing user knowledge of the quantity of greenhouse gasses that certain actions, like driving a car, flying, or eating specific foods, release. The software can inspire users to adopt more environmentally friendly behaviors by empowering them with this knowledge, which will allow them to make better decisions about their everyday activities.
